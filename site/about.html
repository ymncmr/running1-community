Rooted in Local Culture.

Malmong Running Club started with a simple idea: bring the famous Filipino warmth to the running tracks. We blend the high-performance mindset of athletes with the laid-back, joyful spirit of our local communities.

From the streets of BGC to the mountain trails of Baguio, every kilometre we share is a celebration of who we are as a people.

location_on Proudly Philippine Based

The Post-Run Feast.

We believe the best part of the run happens after the run. Sharing stories, laughing over local delicacies, and building bonds that strengthen our community with every shared meal.

From siopao at the finish line to full breakfast spreads after long runs — eating together is part of who we are.
