Quick Answers

Do I need to be fast to join?

Not at all. We have pacing groups for all levels — from first-time joggers to seasoned racers. The focus is on moving forward together.

Is there a membership fee?

Our core weekly community runs are free to join. We offer optional premium membership for specialized coaching and exclusive club gear.

What should I bring to a run?

Just your running shoes and a bottle of water. We provide route maps and pacers. Everything else — community, laughter, and post-run food — is on us.

Can I join if I'm outside Manila?

Absolutely. We have active groups in Cebu and Baguio, and we regularly organize runs in other major Philippine cities. Join and we'll connect you with runners near you.
