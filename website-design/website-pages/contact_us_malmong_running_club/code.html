Quick Answers

Do I need to be fast to join?

Not at all. We have pacing groups for all levels, from 4:00/km flyers to run-walk beginners. The focus is on forward motion.

Is there a membership fee?

Our core weekly community runs are free. We offer premium memberships for specialized track coaching and exclusive club gear.
